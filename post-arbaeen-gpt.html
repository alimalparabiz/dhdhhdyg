Designing Arbaeen GPT: A Compassionate Pilgrimage Guide

August 18, 2025 8 min read
Share Copy Link

First time pilgrims often need help with packing, health basics, and finding services along the route. I built Arbaeen GPT to give clear, kind, and accurate answers in the language each person prefers.

Persona and tone

The assistant greets users with respect, then answers in simple language. The tone is calm and helpful. It offers short lists and direct steps.

Knowledge routing

  • Spiritual topics route to spiritual guides.
  • Health topics route to health and safety guides.
  • Services and locations route to a directory of resources.

Why simplicity wins

In stressful moments, short answers help more than long essays. The assistant uses brief sentences, bold labels, and clear bullets. It also suggests next questions so users can continue without feeling lost.

What it helped with

  • Packing lists for different weather and length of travel.
  • Basic health tips like hydration and foot care.
  • Finding nearby services and mawkibs along the route.

Takeaway

Good assistants serve people by reducing anxiety and effort. Respectful language and clear actions make a real difference for first time visitors.
