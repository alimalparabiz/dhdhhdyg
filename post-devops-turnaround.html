From Firefighting to Profit Center: A DevOps Turnaround

August 10, 2025 6 min read
Share Copy Link

When I took over a DevOps function, it was losing money and fighting constant issues. We changed that with audits, automation, and financial clarity. The same habits now help me ship reliable AI systems.

First steps

  • Automate audits with scripts to find idle resources.
  • Decommission unused sites and servers to stop waste.
  • Match invoices to services to recover missing revenue.

Discipline that transfers to AI

  • Measure everything. Track outcomes and cost.
  • Create repeatable checks. Do not rely on memory.
  • Improve in small steps and publish changes.

Takeaway

Great engineering is not only about shiny tools. It is about simple habits that compound. The same thinking that turns a DevOps unit around also makes AI products stable.
